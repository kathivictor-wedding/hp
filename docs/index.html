Wedding Party
  • Schloss Nymphenburg and Palmenhaus
  • 10 Jun 2023, 17PM - 01AM

We all meet in front of Schloss Nymphenburg and take some pictures in on this hopefully sunny summer afternoon. We then walk together to our party location: the Palmenhaus. Here we are welcomed for an Apero in the garden. After taking our seats inside we start with some introductory words from us to introduce all of you. A three course meal then awaits us, topped off by our grand wedding cake. Later in the evening there will be plenty of time to dance and go crazy!

After a good rest from the party we would love to meet you again for our optional calm picnic in the wonderful Englischer Garten. We will provide some snacks and drinks, but feel free to bring along food to share. The dauntless among you may also take bath in the Eisbach.
